Mission\

We provide short sprints & fast feedback that leave clients with no surprises.

What makes Makapen different? We're devoted to an agile philosophy of delivering constant, meaningful value to our clients. We're a small agency, but we make a huge impact by allowing our clients to prototype, test against real users, and get to market quickly with a viable product. We're used by both startups looking to bootstrap their first product, and large companies looking to explore new strategies.

Learn\

Our goal is to understand your mission, goals, and circumstances so that we can help you make technology decisions that fit your situation. We spend time talking with you and whiteboarding ideas, we bounce ideas off our team, and present our thought and a suggested starting point.

Iterate\

We deliver a fully functioning product starting from Sprint 1! Our process is to identify features and goals per sprint, work on implementation for 2 weeks, and deliver the results to you. We also do weekly demos and keep a constantly open communication channel to make sure that we adopt feedback right away.

We estimate each sprint ahead of time and give you a fixed price so that you know exactly what we will implement and know the price. Because the product is functioning and hosted from the beginning, you can begin testing your software and incorporate the results as you plan next features.
